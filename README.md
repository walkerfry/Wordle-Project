Instructions: 

In this game, you will have an unknown, random word that is five letters long. As you type, the letters will fill from the left side to the right side. Once you enter a word, you will move down to the next line and repeat. You will get six tries to guess what this word is. Each time you guess, the game will tell you if the letter is correct and in the right place, correct but in the wrong place, and incorrect. If the letter is correct and in the right place, the letter will turn green. If the letter is correct but in the wrong place, the letter will turn yellow. If the letter is not in the unknown word, then it will turn grey.

Rules:

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. If you guess the word within your six tries, you win the game. If you are not able to enter the word within your six tries, you lose.
